Places to visit near Lonavala and Khandala
Bhushi Dam

Bhushi Dam, nestled in the serene hills of Lonavala, offers a tranquil escape with its gushing water and lush surroundings. A popular spot for picnics, the dam invites visitors to enjoy the scenic beauty while engaging in water activities and relishing the refreshing atmosphere.

Lion's Point

Providing breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and hills, Lion's Point is a must-visit vantage point in Lonavala. The steep ascent to this viewpoint rewards visitors with a mesmerizing sight of the landscape, especially during the monsoon when the hills are adorned with lush greenery and cascading waterfalls.

Sunset Point

A magical spot to witness the sun bid adieu to the day, Sunset Point in Lonavala offers a spectacular view as the sky transforms into a canvas of vibrant hues. It's a favorite among tourists and locals alike, providing a serene ambiance to unwind and capture the enchanting beauty of the setting sun.

Narayani Dham

Narayani Dham, a divine retreat in Lonavala, is a spiritual haven adorned with intricately designed temples and serene surroundings. Devotees and visitors seek solace in this tranquil abode, where the spiritual atmosphere and architectural beauty create a peaceful sanctuary.

Lohagad Fort

Steeped in history, Lohagad Fort stands as a testament to the region's rich past. The fort's strategic location atop a hill offers commanding views of the landscape, making it a popular trekking destination for history enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Karla Caves

Karla Caves, an ancient Buddhist rock-cut complex, showcases intricate carvings and sculptures dating back to the 2nd century. Nestled amidst greenery, these caves serve as a fascinating archaeological site and a peaceful retreat for those interested in exploring the region's cultural heritage.

Sunil's Wax Museum

Sunil's Wax Museum in Lonavala brings a touch of glamour with its lifelike wax statues of celebrities and historical figures. A unique attraction for art and pop culture enthusiasts, the museum provides an opportunity to get up close and personal with iconic personalities.

Mapro Garden

A paradise for fruit lovers, Mapro Garden is renowned for its strawberry products and lush strawberry fields. Visitors can explore the scenic garden, indulge in delicious treats, and purchase a variety of fruit-based products, making it a delightful stop for food connoisseurs.

Pawana Lake

Nestled amidst Sahyadri hills, Pawana Lake enchants with its serene waters and panoramic views. Ideal for camping, the lakeside offers a tranquil escape, where visitors can immerse themselves in nature's beauty and witness stunning sunrises and sunsets. Adventure seekers can indulge in water sports like boating and kayaking, while history enthusiasts can explore nearby forts like Lohagad, adding a touch of heritage to this picturesque destination.
